Biography

I have had a career spanning both corporate and startup environments, I bring a wealth of expertise in the realms of data analytics and artificial intelligence. My journey began at EY, where I honed my skills as a data analytics consultant, providing invaluable insights to clients. Subsequently, I joined as a data scientist at Workday to work on the augmented analytics product “People Analytics”.

My academic journey has been equally rewarding. Recently, I graduated with first-class honors from the Technological University Dublin, where I earned a Masters in Data Science, further solidifying my proficiency in this dynamic field. Prior to this, I earned a Bachelor’s degree in MSISS from Trinity College Dublin.

As I embark on the next chapter of my career, I am driven by a passion for leveraging cutting-edge technologies, such as AI and augmented analytics, to create impactful solutions in niche domains. Whether as a founder shaping the future of innovation or as a valued member of a forward-thinking analytics team, I am eager to contribute my expertise and drive meaningful change in the industry.
